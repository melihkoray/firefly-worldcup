Coverage Areas

Be Everywhere
Fans Are

From pre-game gatherings to stadium perimeters, Firefly's moving media fills every gap stationary placements can't reach.

Pre-Game Fan Gathering Areas

Reach audiences at pre-game meetups and city gathering points, where venue-adjacent stationary media is limited. Moving media keeps brands present throughout the pre-game journey.

Citywide Fan Zones & Urban Hubs

Fan energy spreads across multiple hubs. Firefly stays visible between distributed fan zones and high-traffic areas, connecting moments throughout and filling the gaps left by stationary placements.

Transit Corridors & Travel Routes

Movement between city centers and stadiums creates high-attention travel corridors. Firefly follows the natural flow of fans along these routes, enabling opportunistic presence in high-energy movement areas.

Game Day Stadium Coverage

Game Day excitement builds as fans head toward the stadium. Crowds fill surrounding streets just outside the secure perimeter. Firefly stays visible in these final steps, carrying city energy into the Game Day experience.
